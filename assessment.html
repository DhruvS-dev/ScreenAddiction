Screen Addiction Self-Assessment

Choose one of the five options In The Following Assessment

*Make sure to answer every question


1) How Often Do You Find yourself Staying Online Longer Than You Intended?



Occasionally




2) How often do You neglect household chores or refuse to help your parents run errands to spend more time online?



Occasionally




3) How Often Do You make new friends online?



Occasionally




4) How Often Do Your friends (both online and offline) point out your excessive screen usage?



Occasionally




5) Do your personal goals, school-work and academics get badly affected due to excessive screen time?



Occasionally




6) How Often Do You check your instagram DMs/Whatsapp messages before doing another task?



Occasionally




7) Do you become Secretive/Sensitive when somone asks you what you do online or on social media?



Occasionally




8) How Often Do You Find yourself Staying Online Longer Than You Intended?



Occasionally




9) How often do you look to going online and watching reels/videos to escape negative thoughts/stress you have in real life?



Occasionally




10) How often do you find yourselflooking forward to going online again?



Occasionally




11) How often do you worry that taking a break from the internet/social media will make you bored?



Occasionally




12) Do you get extremely upset/yell if someone bothers you online?



Occasionally




13) Do you often lose sleep because you are stuck on your phone watching reels/social media?



Occasionally




14) Are you often distracted by thinking of things you watched on the internet, and wish to open your gadget to watch something while doing work?



Occasionally




15) How often do you hide your gadget usage patterns/screentime from friends or family?



Occasionally




16) Do you try to find excuses to use your phone/gadgets?



Occasionally




17) Do you feel moody/under the weather, when you are not watching anything, but feel better when you go back online?



Occasionally




18) Do you often become unaware about how much time has passed, and end up spending hours on your phone?



Occasionally





*Do not feel ashamed or embarrassed to answer truthfully, even if you know that your actions are not good. The important detail is that you be truthful to yourself and accept your flaws 😊
