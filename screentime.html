Measure Your Screen-Time

Open this timer when you are about to watch movies, youtube or do schoolwork on the computer.
In order to reset it, simply refresh the page.
Record your screentime every session to keep track of how long you spend.

**Remember to keep the tab open while watching youtube, movies, etc. For users who want to use a phone, you can open a new tab and keep viewing it while this tab is still open.
