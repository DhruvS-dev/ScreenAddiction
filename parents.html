Parents' Role In Managing Children's Screentime

To ensure that the younger generation does not grow up completely surrounded by the omline world, parents must take care of the cchild's screen habits and prevent excessive exposure to screens.

This can be done in several ways:

  • Restricting the child's screentime by putting a password lock- this allows the parents to control how much screentime they give to the child.

  • Parents can start to socialise with the child as an alternative to socialising online. As a plus, this makes family bonds much closer.

  • Parents should refrain from buying their children phones, Ipads or gaming consoles at a very young age.

  • Encourage the child to spend more time outside and make friends within the locality.

  • Help the child understand why excessive screen usage is harmful at a younger age.

  • DO NOT give gadgets to kids/ toddlers as a means of distraction. It can cause much more harm than good.
